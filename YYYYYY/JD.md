About the job


Overview


Webmark is looking for a Full-Stack Developer with 1–2 years of professional experience who enjoys building real products, learning fast, and working with modern development tools.



This role is ideal for someone who is comfortable working with React and Node.js, enjoys solving real engineering problems, and wants to grow quickly in a team that ships products frequently.



You will work on production systems, internal tools, and SaaS products, contributing across both frontend and backend development.







Key Responsibilities


• Build and maintain web applications using React and Node.js

• Implement new features across frontend, backend, and APIs

• Participate in fast, iterative development cycles with frequent deployments

• Collaborate with developers and product teams to design scalable, reliable systems

• Use AI-assisted development tools to accelerate coding, debugging, and testing

• Write clean, maintainable code that follows modern engineering practices

• Debug issues, improve performance, and continuously refine the codebase







Required Skills


• 1–2 years of professional development experience

• Strong proficiency in React and Node.js

• Good understanding of JavaScript or TypeScript

• Familiarity with REST APIs and backend development concepts

• Experience with Git and modern development workflows

• Strong debugging and problem-solving skills

• Ability to quickly learn new systems and technologies



We do not expect perfection. What matters most is curiosity, strong fundamentals, and a willingness to learn.







Preferred (Nice to Have)


• Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or similar)

• Familiarity with serverless, Docker, or modern deployment workflows

• Experience working with databases such as PostgreSQL or MongoDB

• Exposure to AI-assisted development tools







What You’ll Gain


• Hands-on experience building real production systems and SaaS products

• Exposure to modern full-stack architectures and rapid deployment cycles

• A fast-paced environment where you will learn quickly and grow your engineering skills
